Rock, Paper, or Scissors!

In the game of Rock, Paper, Scissors,
players simultaneously choose one of three hand gestures (rock, paper, or scissors), with rock beating scissors, scissors beating paper, and paper beating rock, and a tie if both players choose the same.

Rules of this game:

  • Rock beats Scissors: Rock smashes or breaks scissors.
  • Scissors beats Paper: Scissors cut paper.
  • Paper beats Rock: Paper covers rock.
